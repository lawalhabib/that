Moy Pui Yee

Moy Pui Yee
  • Independent Non-Executive Director
  • Member of Risk Management & Sustainability Committee

Ms Moy Pui Yee, 56, a Malaysian, is an Independent Non-Executive Director of Sime Darby Berhad. She was appointed to the Board on 2 July 2018.

Ms Moy is currently a partner and Co-head of the Mergers & Acquisition Practice Group of Rahmat Lim & Partners and has been in active legal practice since 1992.Her practice focus is on take-overs, mergers and acquisitions (both private and public), joint ventures, foreign investments, and corporate commercial transactions. She has been involved in transactions across a wide range of industries including financial services, energy and power, manufacturing, retail and consumer, hospitality and plantations. She is concurrently a Partner in Allen & Gledhill LLP, the firm’s associate firm in Singapore.

Ms Moy graduated in Economics in 1988 and in Law in 1990 from Monash University, Australia. She was admitted to the Malaysian Bar in 1992.
