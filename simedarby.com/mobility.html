Mobility at Sime Darby

Change is the only constant. If the invention of the wheel transformed the way humanity moved, a similar shift is already in motion. 

Sime Darby is not content to stay on the sidelines. We are intent on standing side by side with our world-class partners to play an active role in the future of mobility. We aim to collaborate with and enable key players in developing innovative mobility solutions and help put them into action.

 

The way we move will change. Our dedication to serving you is constant.

Technological advances, evolving consumer habits and changing regulations are altering the mobility landscape. Sime Darby has embarked on a journey of discovery on how best to continue serving the needs and wants of our partners and customers, with a focus on electric vehicle adoption and shared mobility.

Leader in EV
For all your Electric Vehicle needs

We aim to be the leading Electric Vehicle specialist in the region through the assembly, distribution and retail of Electric Vehicles, as well as by playing an active role in the charging infrastructure value chain.
Preferred Fleet Solution Provider
Turnkey fleet solution provider

We strive to be the preferred one-stop fleet service provider. We supply vehicles via various ownership schemes, provide maintenance including mobile servicing, monitoring and predictive maintenance.

 

Partner and Enabler for the Future of Mobility

We aim to be an agent of change to shift both our partners and customers into the new era of mobility. We have begun our journey to deliver innovative solutions through collaborations with key players in the mobility sector. 

 

Enabling the shift to Electric Vehicles

 

Sime Darby Berhad to Play a Key Role in the Nation’s EV ambitions

Sime Darby Motors at the forefront of EV infrastructure rollout for Malaysia

 

Complementing Malaysia's green mobility agenda

We are collaborating with Malaysian Green Technology and Climate Change Corporation and Ecoride to support the implementation of the Malaysian government’s Low Carbon Mobility Blueprint
 

Development of a highly efficient EV charging network

We have teamed up with Tenaga Nasional Bhd and PLUS Expressways Bhd to set up EV charging stations along highways across Peninsular Malaysia 

Making EV Charging Easy and Accessible

Charging Your Way Forward

 

Solutions to suit your EV Charging needs

KINETA completes Sime Darby’s offering of an end-to-end solution for the EV market via the supply and installation of EV chargers and the provision of EV charging solutions in Malaysia

Fleet management partner for leading Mobility-as-a-Service players

An investor and partner to SOCAR Mobility Malaysia since 2018 in new car and used car sales, fleet management and mobile after-sales service.

Sime Darby Berhad Accelerates Mobility Strategy

 

Fleet leasing and supply

We lease cars to SOCAR Mobility Malaysia which are listed on their platform
 

Tailored and convenient after-sales service

We provide mobile after-sales solutions for SOCAR Mobility Malaysia's vehicles off-site

Digitalising new and used car sales experience

   

 

Sime Darby Auto Selection To Collaborate with myTukar to Enhance Customer Experience

 

Enhancing pre-owned car trading experience with Carro 

Furthering our investment in Carro, SEA’s largest car marketplace, we are partnering with them to improve used car trading and big data analytics on used car transactions
 

Preparing for car buying experiences of the future

We have invested in Carwow, the leading European one-stop online car marketplace to futureproof our business.
 

Omnichannel transformation

We developed a 3-year omnichannel blueprint to create seamless customer journeys.

Investments in Disruptive Mobility Companies

   

 

 

Investing in pioneers in electrification and mobility infrastructure 

We have invested in GLy’s New Mobility Fund, a mobility-focused venture capital fund whose anchor investors are Geely Group and SK Holdings. We aim to leverage on the fund’s investment and industry expertise, resources and network to accelerate Sime Darby’s mobility agenda

Delivering Sustainable Futures

Mobility @ Sime Darby aims to play an active role in developing a future for mobility that is sustainable and inclusive.
