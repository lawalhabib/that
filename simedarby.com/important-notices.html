Important Notices


Fraudulent Proposals
It has come to the attention of SIME DARBY BERHAD that there have been incidents of individuals, entities and/or companies claiming to represent Sime Darby Berhad and/or its group of companies (“Sime Darby Group”), as employees, agents and/or representatives offering business proposals. These individuals, entities and/or companies are offering fraudulent business proposals to the public. The proposals include offers for investments, tender contracts and/or possible opportunities for partnership, collaboration and/or projects with the Sime Darby Group. 

The public should be wary of individuals, entities and companies making such business proposals. You should seek further clarification and verification by directly contacting Sime Darby Berhad immediately. The Sime Darby Group shall not be responsible to any persons, parties, entities, firms or companies who suffer or incur any losses or damages as a result of entering into such fraudulent business proposals.

If in doubt, please send your queries to: communications@simedarby.com or whistleblowing@simedarby.com
 

Website
Sime Darby Berhad wishes to alert members of the public that the website simedarby-my.com and simedarby.hk are neither owned, operated nor maintained by Sime Darby.

Kindly be guided that the official websites of Sime Darby Berhad and its associated business divisions are as follows:-
•    Sime Darby Berhad: www.simedarby.com
•    Sime Darby Industrial: www.simedarbyindustrial.com
•    Sime Darby Motors: www.simedarbymotors.com

Do not trust any hyperlink that you may receive through e-mail. When visiting our websites, we strongly advise you to always check and confirm that the url address in your internet browser is correct. This is to ensure that you are not redirected to the wrong sites. Sime Darby Berhad has communicated with the authorities on this matter.


Email Employment Scam
Sime Darby Berhad wishes to alert members of the public that there had been a spate of e-mails allegedly offering employment with the Sime Darby group of companies. The e-mails contain request for personal information and payment for the processing the job applications or offer. PLEASE BE ADVISED THAT THEY ARE E-MAIL SCAMS.

Sime Darby Berhad, its subsidiaries and related companies do not offer make job offers through e-mails without any interview. Neither Sime Darby Berhad nor any of its group companies have appointed third party to send out offers of employment on its behalf.

Sime Darby Berhad and its group of companies also do not request for any payment or impose any charges to process job offers nor has it allowed any third parties to collect such payment.

Please do not send any personal information nor make any payments pursuant to such “offer of employment”. Do not trust any hyperlink that you may receive through e-mail. When visiting Sime Darby Berhad’s or any of its group companies’ websites, we strongly advise you to always check the url in your internet browser is correct.


Social Media Recruitment Scam
It has come to the attention of Sime Darby Berhad that unauthorised individuals or organisations have been falsely using the Sime Darby name and logo on their websites and on social media networks claiming to recruit on behalf of Sime Darby to obtain email addresses from interested job-seekers or members of the public. PLEASE BE ADVISED THAT SIME DARBY HAS NOT AUTHORISED ANY INDIVIDUAL OR ORGANISATION TO RECRUIT ON ITS BEHALF. THESE ATTEMPTS ARE SCAMS.

Sime Darby Berhad, its subsidiaries and related companies do not offer or make job offers through third-party websites and social media other than through the links listed below. 

Sime Darby Berhad wishes to stress that all job vacancies in Sime Darby are posted on the Sime Darby Career Centre.

Any other recruitment websites posting job vacancies related to Sime Darby, either on their websites or on social media networks should be treated with caution. We appeal to the public not to respond to such postings.

Please do not send any personal information pursuant to such “job openings”. Do not trust any hyperlink that you may receive through emails and on social media networks. When visiting Sime Darby Berhad’s or any of its group companies’ websites, we strongly advise you to always check that the URL in your internet browser is correct.

 

Procurement Project Scam
There have been attempts by unidentified individuals claiming to represent Sime Darby Berhad, falsely offering opportunities to participate in a procurement project and requesting for company credentials while misusing the Sime Darby Berhad name.

These fraudulent messages offering procurement opportunities are being sent via email from intldesk@simedarbyberhad.com. Sime Darby Berhad has not appointed any individuals, entities, or companies to offer such opportunities.

Members of the public should seek further clarification and verification by contacting us either via email at communications@simedarby.com or whistleblowing@simedarby.com or via telephone at +603-76232000
